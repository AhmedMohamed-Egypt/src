About Us

Canny Vision is a Canadian startup delivering customizable AI solutions in motion detection, object classification, behavior tracking, incident detection, and traffic monitoring. With a strong commitment to quality, privacy, and security, our team of experts harnesses the latest advancements in Cognitive Vision and Video Analytics to provide reliable, scalable solutions across key industries.

Focused exclusively on the Canadian market, Canny Vision stands at the forefront of video analytics technology, helping organizations elevate their security, efficiency, and intelligence.
